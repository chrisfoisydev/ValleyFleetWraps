FAQs

Straight answers for business owners.

How much does a commercial fleet wrap cost?

It depends on vehicle type, wrap coverage, and fleet size. Lettering packages cost less than partial wraps, and partial wraps cost less than full wraps. Send photos of your vehicles and we'll give you a clear quote — no obligation.

Do I need a full wrap or partial wrap?

Not every fleet needs full coverage. Many service businesses get excellent results from a well-designed partial wrap or lettering system. Your free audit includes a recommendation based on your vehicles and budget.

Can you match our existing brand?

Yes. We work from your logo, colors, and any brand guidelines you have. If your brand needs cleanup to work well at vehicle scale, we'll tell you exactly what to adjust.

Can you wrap one vehicle first and the rest later?

Yes, and we recommend it for many fleets. We build your layout as a template, so every future vehicle matches the first one.

How long does installation take?

Most vans and trucks take 1–3 days depending on coverage. For fleets, we schedule vehicles in phases so your crews stay on the road.

Do you wrap trailers and box trucks?

Yes. Trailers and box trucks are some of the best advertising surfaces a service business owns — large, flat, and highly visible in traffic and on job sites.

What information should be on a service vehicle?

At minimum: your service category, phone number, company name, and service area. License numbers where required. The goal is that a homeowner can understand what you do and how to reach you in about three seconds.

Can you help redesign an outdated fleet?

Yes — that's exactly what our Fleet Refresh package is for. We audit what you have, recommend a cleaner system, and phase the updates so you're never off the road.

Do you work with new businesses?

Absolutely. A professional wrap on day one helps a new company look established from the first service call.

Do you serve the whole Valley?

Yes. We're based in Deer Valley and work with fleets everywhere in Greater Phoenix — Glendale, Peoria, Scottsdale, Mesa, Tempe, Chandler, Gilbert, Queen Creek, Surprise, Goodyear, and everywhere between. If your trucks drive the Valley, we'll come to you.
